I’m a theoretical physics grad student at U.C. Berkeley, trying to understand general aspects of quantum dynamics. In the past I’ve also worked on the biophysics of melanoma cancer cell transitions, and on the solid state physics of some rather special crystals that can only exist in 6, 7, or 8 dimensional spaces. I graduated from Caltech with a B.S. in Physics in 2018.
